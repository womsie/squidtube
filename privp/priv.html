Privacy Policy for SquidTube

At SquidTube, user privacy is highly prioritized. This Privacy Policy demonstrates the types of personal info we collect and how we use, disclose and protect that information.

Information We Collect

We gather personal information which you provide to us during account registration, when you upload content, or as you interact with our services. This could include your name, email address, videos uploaded, or usernames.

Additionally, we collect information automatically as you use SquidTube, involving the apps, browsers or devices you may use to access SquidTube services, as well as technical information such as browser settings/type, operating system, unique identifiers (string of characters used to uniquely identify a browser, app or device) and IP address.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Personalize your experience on SquidTube
  • Analyze usage trends and gather feedback to enhance our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent or at your direction
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our services and conducting our business
  • To comply with legal obligations, respond to legal requests, or protect our rights and the rights of others
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children's Privacy

SquidTube is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website. Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at squidtube@gmail.com.
